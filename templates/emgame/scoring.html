Scoring for EMgame is described below


Group stage
Predict the outcome of the groups! You get 2 points per team that qualifies for the 'round of 16' (position is irrelevant). However, correctly predicting the order of all four teams in a group will net you 5 bonus points.

Teams advancing out of the round of 16
Each team that is correctly predicted reaching this level earns you 4 points.

Quarterfinals
Each correct team is worth 8 points.

Semifinals
Each correct team is worth 16 points.

2016 Euro Champion
Correct team is worth 32 points.

Strikers
Choose three players and get 2 bonus points for every* goal they score.
* According to the official FIFA stats.

Defensive team
This team will give you 3 fresh points everytime they end a game with no goals scored against them (includes extra-time if needed).

Picks must be entered prior to kickoff of the first match!
